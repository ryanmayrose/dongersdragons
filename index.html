About the League

Dongers & Dragons is a fantasy-lite baseball league built around one sacred truth: spend your dongs wisely.
8 Hitters Team scoring is based on 2026 MLB home runs by your rostered hitters.
1 Pitcher Pitchers are tracked separately for no-hitter and perfect-game glory.
150 Dong Budget Draft wisely. Every dong spent matters.
Live Scoring Rosters come from your Google Sheet. HR totals come from MLB stats.
